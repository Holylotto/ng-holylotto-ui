Why 20% on everything?

When there are many players, in each category the prize is divided among more people. That is, how many more numbers you hit, the more you win.

RANDOMNESS.

We take the winning combination of the last 5 digits of an Ethereum hash function. When the draw closes we select the next Ethereum block that gets mined. The hash is a combination of numbers and letters impossible to predict, it is used for the Blockchain operation and security.

TICKETS.

DISTRIBUTION.

The funds collected from ticket sales are distributed in the next way:

70% goes to the Prize Pool and Jackpot.

20% The holy maintenance and 10% to ensure the game security.

JACKPOT.

20% of the Prize pool is destined to the Jackpot pool, if there is a winner he gets 20% of the total prize pool plus the accumulated Jackpot (in case there is), if there are more than one winner the 20% gets divided into the number of winner there are and if nobody wins the jackpot (guessing 5 digits) it gets accumulated for the next drawing.

PRIZE.

If there are more than one winner the 20% corresponding to the guessed numbers is divided by the number of winners in the category. If there are no winners, the sum is rolled over to the Jackpot pool for the next drawing.Agreement
